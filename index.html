Benefits

Our company has been producing inflatable boats under the registered TM ΩMega and all the necessary components for over 20 years. During this time, we have managed to gain enough experience to satisfy the needs of both a fan of quiet fishing in closed waters and a supporter of extreme movement across water spaces. We are always ready to provide our customers with the widest selection of exceptionally high-quality products.

Here you can buy inflatable boats that have proven themselves and are loved by many, as well as new items from our developments - boats of the Delta series and inflatable kayaks. Always remembering our customers, we try to satisfy your wishes for a comfortable stay as much as possible. We constantly improve our knowledge and apply advanced technologies, producing high-quality inflatable boats at affordable prices. The result of our strict adherence to the highest quality standards is the ever-increasing demand for our products both on the territory of Ukraine and abroad. All ΩMega inflatable boats are manufactured by the Register of Shipping of Ukraine, certificate number SVP-412-3-14-19. Our boats are also certified by the European Union according to directive 94/25/EU and directive 2013/53/EU.
