  • boats Carp seria

    Since 2017, our company has begun the production of inflatable boats using a new technology for the French company GOLD BAITS. Now we are pleased to offer these boats for the native Ukrainian market under the name CARP CERIES. The main difference between these boats and all the others presented on the Ukrainian market is the combined seam technology. (you can read more about the seams used for the manufacture of boats in the article ... .. link to the article about the seams ...

    The difference between this seam and other manufacturers is the presence of a soldered high-density tape. This is not just a strip of the material from which the boat is made, but a special strip for soldering, and is already closed on top with a strip of material. This not only ensured absolute tightness and strength of the connection, but also made it possible to use materials that are not available to other manufacturers. More elastic and durable, these materials are not only very hard to tear, they are even difficult to scratch. The boats of this series are made of Finnish fabric with a density of 1100 g/m2 (FISHER sub-series) or from German Valmex with a density of 1200 g/m2 (PRO sub-series). Also the lower part of the boat is protected by a 120mm PVC profile.
